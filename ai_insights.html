Daily Analysis

Today's habit status plus the last 7 days for trend context. Gemma gives a daily summary, one weekly insight, and one tip for tomorrow. Expected time: 20 – 40 seconds.

Data sent to Gemma: today's habit list with completion status, daily scores for the last 7 days, and per-habit consistency counts for the week.

Weekly Analysis

Full day-by-day breakdown of the last 7 days with per-habit completion rates and category scores. Gemma identifies the key pattern and recommends one priority for next week. Expected time: 30 – 60 seconds.

Data sent to Gemma: daily scores and counts for 7 days, per-habit completion rates, logged totals for measurable habits, and category-level averages.

Monthly Analysis

30-day aggregated stats — completion rates, streaks, best/weakest day of the week per habit, and a first-half vs second-half trend comparison. Expected time: 60 – 90 seconds.

Data sent to Gemma: per-habit completion rates, current and longest streaks, total logged values, best and weakest day of week, and a first-half vs second-half trend score.
