Overview

These summary cards cover your current system shape, tracked history, and recent execution quality.

Insights

Plain-language signals from your recent data: momentum, reliability, best rhythm, growth pockets, and possible tradeoffs.

Positive Correlations

Habits that tend to rise together over the last 90 days of available activity.

Correlation is a clue, not proof. Use this to ask better questions, not to assume causation.

Tradeoff Watch

Habits that tend to move in opposite directions and may be competing for attention or time.

A negative pattern can mean conflict, timing friction, or simply that two habits live on different kinds of days.

Window Intelligence

These cards use each habit's primary window only, so repeated reminders do not double-count the same logged time.

Window Trend Reports

Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly trend lines for planned, logged, unused, and unplanned time in each window. Repeated habits use saved window allocations when available; older repeated history without allocations falls back to the primary window.

Planned time is the targeted time inside that window. Unplanned time is the remaining open capacity in the window. Unused time is the time left on the table after the window closed.

Category Reports

Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly bars for each habit, grouped by category. Bars show actual output and the line shows the target for that period.
